## Summary

The ETH/BTC Ratio Rotation Skill monitors the ETH/BTC ratio to identify whether market leadership is favoring ETH or BTC.

It is designed as a simple, transparent, and reviewable example for the CWC AI Trading Skill Challenge.

## Key Idea

- ETH/BTC rising: ETH is gaining relative strength.
- ETH/BTC falling: BTC is gaining relative strength.
- ETH/BTC moving sideways: no clear rotation signal.
